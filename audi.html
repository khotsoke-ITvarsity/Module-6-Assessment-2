Audi A3 Sedan


Audi has officially unveiled the A3 sedan of the new (second in a row) generation. The changes in the exterior of the car were mostly evolutionary, and from all angles the four-door "a-three" remained recognizable.

Up front, the new 2021 Audi A3 features a massive honeycomb hexagonal grille and an aggressive bumper with silver accents on the side air intakes. Plus, the car has "fanged" LED optics and a muscular hood.
